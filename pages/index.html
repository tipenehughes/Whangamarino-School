August 12th 2020

COVID-19


We are at level 2 covid-19 from 12th August 2020. At this stage we are still allowing parents and whanau onsite but would encourage you to not enter classrooms unless you absolutely have to. If you have a mask to wear whilst picking your children up or dropping your children off at kura we would encourage it. Children may wear a mask to kura as long as they are not distracted by their use (masks are not compulsory). If you prefer to keep your tamariki at home for level 2 you are able to make this choice. Attendance is only compulsory at level 1.
We will keep you up to date if anything changes.
